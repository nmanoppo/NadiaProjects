Welcome to the About Page!

Learn more about BookWorld today, and why we exist!

BookWorld was founded in 2012 by our founder Nadia Manoppo.

She wanted a place where people of all ages can be in an environment where they can read and study books.

BookWorld is meant to offer its customers a modern Bookstore/Coffee shop experience, where they can read and buy coffee and books as they wish.

We provide wifi access so that you may stay connected with the world while you read, over 1,800+ books are available, and good quality coffee beans to fuel your energy.
